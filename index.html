Our story

Born six years ago in the heart of North Amsterdam, our team is more than just a club; it's a global family. We bring together 20 talented players from 20 different nations, united by a passion for high-level football and a commitment to human values. On the pitch, we play with skill and determination. Off the pitch, we stand for causes greater than ourselves, using our shared voice to advocate for justice and freedom. We are not just a team; we are a movement, proving that diversity is our greatest strength.

Our Sponsors

While we play at an amateur level, our standards are high. Joining X&3K requires solid skills and dedication to the game. Every player commits to putting the team first, fostering unity both on and off the pitch. With members from across the globe, tolerance and open-mindedness aren't just values—they're our foundation. We celebrate diversity, embrace different perspectives, and believe that respect transcends borders. At X&3K, we're eclectic by nature, and that's what makes us stronger together.
